Syed who?

Syed is game for breaking boundaries, building bridges, crossing oceans of irrelevance -- one breath and one word at a time.

He is a foot soldier on a mission and a movement to create a content universe envisaged by the Subject Matter Expert (SME).

Generally, a technical writer strives:

  • to remain committed to the SME
  • to establish the rules of the SME in the land of the SME
  • to clash with confusion, exterminate content gaps, and weed out any room for negative user experience
  • to reach out to the SME for a helping hand to complete the content universe
  • to capture all the scenarios with whatever level of knowledge possessed
  • to remain committed to address the complex issues faced by the society
  • to believe that burden does not go beyond his capacity and that there is no room for excuse
  • to start barefoot, seek help, and build on the content, brick by brick
  • to absorb whatever information she gets
  • to remain unmoved if most SMEs and other sources close doors on her face
  • to appreciate even if only few voices come forward for knowledge sharing
  • to believe that documentation is not an easy task
  • to not spend excessive time learning about the product, but quickly shifts to end-to-end user journey
  • to believe that empty words are part of the problem
  • to not believe in just desserts
  • to believe help cannot be extracted by force, but what comes voluntarily
  • to believe that this mission is not for few days, months, or years... it's eternal
  • to document whatever knowledge has been gained
  • to believe that there is no ideal, sweet time for documentation
  • to keep promise of comprehensive documentation and does not hesitate to seek help from SMEs at any time
  • to remain motivated
  • to start documentation early
  • to borrow thoughts from anyone involved
  • to turn losses into wins by putting trust in documentation
  • to believe that SMEs will remain firm and cooperative in their support to documentation
  • to believe that she is not knowledgeful about the product she is documenting
  • to provide service that benefits people
  • Not for material or personal profit
  • to provide a sense of security through her brute force of competence
  • to consider that people have the right to know the product like the back of their hand
  • to believe that she is not the boss
  • to believe that misinformation and information scarcity has no place
  • to believe that it is HER time and she is duty bound to listen to the SME
  • to believe that her job is to make sure the SMEs are heard and respected
  • to believe that HER COMMITMENT IS TO LISTEN TO THE SME
  • to believe that the SME IS RESPONSIBLE for the success or failure of the mission
  • to believe that SME knows the rules and the regulations of using the product
  • to lead documentation with new thoughts and ideas
  • to decry old methods and ideas
  • to believe that SHE is temporary, but SME is forever
  • to remain responsible for anything but clear communication
  • to believe that a product should neither be insulted nor feared, and it should be explored and used fully.
  • to spot content gaps lying in the open (Nobody can get away by burying their head in the sand.)
